Why does this work? Active Recall.

Under the hood, our bodies are designed to conserve energy. The neural pathways we use get stronger, and the ones we don't get deleted. Re-reading your notes or writing down answers while staring at open solutions is mostly a waste of time because you aren't forcing your brain to do the work. You're just transcribing.

To actually learn, you need Active Recall: closing the book, putting away the solutions, and retrieving the information entirely from memory. Combine that with Spaced Repetition (doing it multiple times over spaced intervals), and it fundamentally changes how you retain information.

That is exactly what this hub is designed for. Each tool forces you to pull the answer from memory and provides instant feedback, allowing you to correct mistakes in real-time and bridge the gap between passive reading and active learning.
